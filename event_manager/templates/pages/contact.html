Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the page. Fill in your details, choose your role (Event Organizer, Venue Manager, or Admin), and follow the verification steps. You'll be ready to start within minutes!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for venue bookings. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.

How do I list my venue on Horizon Planners?

Sign up as a Venue Manager, complete your profile with venue details, upload photos and amenities, set pricing, and define availability. Our team will verify your listing before it goes live.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days or more before the event date are fully refundable. Cancellations made 3-7 days before are refunded 50%. Cancellations within 72 hours are non-refundable. Specific terms may vary by venue.
