Privacy Policy

Horizon Planners is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

This policy applies to all Horizon Planners users, including event organizers, venue managers, attendees, and platform administrators. It covers information collected through our website, mobile applications, and related services.

Event Information

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, and destruction.

Security Measures
